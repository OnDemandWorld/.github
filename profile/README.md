## Why ODW·AI

Small businesses have stopped experimenting with AI — they're buying. The typical SME now runs a handful of disconnected AI tools, each with its own login, its own bill, and its own opaque cloud pipeline sending your data somewhere you can't see.

We think that's backwards. **ODW·AI is one open engine with ten focused modules that share the same authentication, orchestration, and privacy boundary** — so they compose into multi-step agents instead of piling up as yet another SaaS subscription.

And because the whole stack is open source, you can run it on your own infrastructure, inspect every line, extend anything, and keep every byte of your data inside your boundary.

> The moat isn't the model. It's *where the data lives* — and who controls it.

## Why We Give the Code Away

**Free to self-host.** Clone it, run it on your own servers, keep your data in your boundary. No license fee, no per-seat tax, no usage meters.

**Inspect every line.** No black boxes. Audit how your AI handles customer data, see exactly which models run, extend anything you need.

**Bring your own models.** Open models (Llama, Mistral, Qwen) run locally, with optional cloud burst when *you* decide it's appropriate.

**We earn our keep on services** — deployment, customization, vertical configuration, and support — not by holding your data hostage.

---

## Built for Trust & Compliance

Every module shares one data boundary and one audit trail — not ten policies to reconcile. Designed from day one to address GDPR, PDPO, PDPA and the EU AI Act, for SMBs in regulated industries.
